The Label

We press
music that
refuses to
blend in.

Sontra Records is an independent electronic label working at the edge of club music and the experimental fringe. We exist for the records that don't fit a playlist slot — the ones built for a dark room, a big system, and people who actually listen.

We started the way most good things start: a handful of friends, a shared hard drive of unreleased tracks, and the conviction that the music deserved a home that wasn't optimized for retention metrics. No A&R committees. No focus groups. Just artists we believe in and a commitment to release their work with care.

Every release is mastered for the floor and the headphones alike, sold directly through Bandcamp so the people who make the music keep the larger share of what you pay. We don't chase virality. We chase records that still sound right in ten years.
